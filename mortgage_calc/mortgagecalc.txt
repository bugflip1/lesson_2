This is brain storming/pseudocode for the mortgage calc assignment

3 input information:
- loan amount
- APR
- loan duration

Need to calculate:

- Monthly interest rate

Formula in python is this:

m = p * (j / (1 - (1 + j) ** (-n)))

monthly payment = loan amount x (monthly interest rate / (1 - ( 1 + monthly interest rate) ** (- loan duration in months)))

m = monthly payment
p = loan amount
j = monthly interest rate
n = loan duration in months
